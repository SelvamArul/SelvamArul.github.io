Arul Selvam Periyasamy

I am a Research Scientist in Edge Computing at Siemens AG. Previously, I earned my Ph.D. at the University of Bonn within the Autonomous Intelligent Systems group, led by Prof. Dr. Sven Behnke. I also hold a Master’s in Computer Science from the same institution. As a member of Team NimbRo and Team NimbRo Picking, I have contributed to developing award-winning robots that achieved success in several international competitions. Explore my list of publications, robotics competition history, or CV for more details.
